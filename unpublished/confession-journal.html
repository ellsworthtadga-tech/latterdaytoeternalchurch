The Ignatian Daily Examen — a brief evening review of your day in God's presence.

Step 1
Gratitude
Place yourself in God's presence. Review your day and give thanks for the gifts you received.
Step 2
Petition
Ask the Holy Spirit to help you see your day clearly and honestly.
Step 3
Review
Walk through your day hour by hour. Where did you feel God's presence? Where did you turn away?
Step 4
Respond
Speak to God about what arose. Ask forgiveness where needed. Give thanks where grace was given.
Step 5
Resolve
Look ahead to tomorrow. Ask for God's help with one specific intention.

Guide to Confession
📖
Step-by-Step Guide
How to go to confession
▶
📋 Before You Go
1

Examine Your Conscience

Prayerfully review your actions since your last confession. Use the Examine tab.

2

Pray for Grace

Ask the Holy Spirit for clarity and true contrition.

⛪ During Confession
3

Greeting

The priest greets you. Both make the Sign of the Cross.

4

Confess Your Sins

Begin: "Bless me, Father, for I have sinned. It has been [time] since my last confession."

5

Listen to the Priest

He may offer counsel and will assign a penance.

6

Act of Contrition

Pray the Act of Contrition when prompted.

7

Absolution

The priest prays absolution. You respond: "Amen."

🙏 After Confession
8

Complete Your Penance

Do the assigned penance as soon as possible.

9

Give Thanks

Thank God for His mercy and resolve to amend your life.
